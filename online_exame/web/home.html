About Us

How is this site helpful to institutes/Organization?

This site is a medium by which institutes/Organizations can conduct online exams. They can register on the site and then can enter the exam details and questions. The institutes also provide a list of students that are eligible for the exam.

Welcome to Online Exam System

This Web site provides a facility to the institutes/Organization to host an online test. The online test can be customized according to the requirements of the institute/Organization. An institute/Organization who intents to conduct an online test can register here.

OBJECTIVE

To Provide Facility To Institutes/Organization To Host Online Tests/Exams

No Need To Get A New Software Every time To Conduct An Online Test. Instead Just Register On The Web site And Conduct Any Test.

To Enable Students To Give Exams Online And View Their Results

Saves Time Of Going to Far Away Exam Center Need Not Wait For The Result.
